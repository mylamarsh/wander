Airlines

Choosing the right airline can be tricky. Here are some well-known airlines and a few key points about them to help you out.

Airline Baggage Policies In-flight Prices
Delta Charges for bags on most flights but has a straightforward policy. Good seating and entertainment, as well as complementary snacks. Pricier but widely considered worth it.
Southwest Allows two free checked bags. Free snacks and drinks, not much in-flight entertainment. Competitive pricing with great value for baggage policies.
American Charges for checked bags but has good benefits for frequent fliers. Used to have inadequate in-flight services but has improved, although experiences vary. Prices vary, good deals but many extra fees.
United Charges for checked baggage, fees can very. Varies depending on route and aircraft, generally adequate. Similar to American.
