Hotels

A good nights rest is always important when traveling. Here are some hotels and a few key points about them to help you find the perfect place to recharge.

Hotels Prices Customer Service Variety
Marriot Moderate to high, but as it is a large chain it varies by location and brand. Renowned for high-quality service with emphasis on guest satisfaction and loyalty. Varies from luxury to mid-range to extended stay.
InterContinental Higher prices with luxury offerings, good for travelers seeking upscale experiences. Excellent customer service with attentvie staff and personalized services. Focuses on luxury accomadations in prime locations, known for high-end services and amenities.
Best Western Typically lower rates, prices vary by location. Can be hit or miss depending on the location, as many properties are independently owned and may not have adequate services. Wide variety from budget to mid-range, often tailored to local markets.
Hilton Competitive rates espescially for members, prices vary significantly based on location and brand. Ratings generally very good, with many locations offering personalized services. Offers a diverse range from luxury to budget.
