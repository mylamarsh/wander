Rental Cars

Selecting the perfect rental car company can be tricky. Here are some wide-spread companies and a few key points about them to help you out.

Company Prices Customer Service Vehicles
Enterprise Competitve pricing with discounts for weekend rentals and a good selection of vehicles. Consistently ranks high in customer satisfaction, they emphasize personal service and convenience. Offers a broad selection of varying vehicles.
Hertz Prices vary widely depending on location and demand, offere frequent promotions. Generally good service, but varies by location. Extensive fleet with many options.
Avis Moderate price range, offer deals for corporate travelers and have frequent promotions. Reviews are mixed, depending heaviy on location. Reservations are easy on their app and website. Wide range, although they offer specialty rentals they focus more on mainstream options.
Budget Often has the lowest prices as the name suggests, focusing on economy vehicles. Ratings are average, with a few reports of long wait times and less attentive service. Primarily focused on economy cars and smaller vehicles, with a few exceptions.
