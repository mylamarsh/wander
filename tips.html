Travel Tips

Pack lightly. One of the most annoying things that can happen on a trip is having to carry around tons of baggage. Make sure to pack only the necessities and a few comforts to make your trip stress-free and easy.

Have important documents close by. Make sure to keep your passports, visas, and other necessary paperwork close by just in case. Losing these items could result in a cancelled or delayed trip, especially during international travel.

Do your homework. Before traveling to another country, it is important to get to know the culture. Some things in your culture you may do without even thinking about it, but in other countries it may be mean something else or could even be offensive. Make sure to learn at least a little bit about the country and culture before traveling.
