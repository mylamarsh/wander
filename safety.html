Safety

    Travel.state.gov: The U.S. Department of State provides travel advisories, safety tips, and information about health and security in various countries.

    For real-world insights and personal experiences from other travelers, these forums can provide practical advice and tips about specific destinations.

    Known for its comprehensive medical and security services, this site is excellent if you need in-depth risk assessments and emergency assistance.
